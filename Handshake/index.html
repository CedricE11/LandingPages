Handshake For Organizers

Are you an event organizer or run a booth at a conference? Have you wondered how effective the event is at connecting the right people? Have you wondered how interested any given contact actually is in your product?

Handshake provides an integrated management tool that allows you to track attendees and to see how people are connecting. Our solution uses heuristics to rank contacts according to their level of interest, allowing you to pursue the most promising leads first.

Handshake For Users

Have you ever left an event wishing you had met and retained the contacts of more people? Have you ever fumbled around with business cards instead of focusing on a valuable discussion? Have you ever struggled to manage the pile of business cards you've received after an event?

Handshake allows you to easily find and meet the most relevant people, generating stronger, longer-lasting relationships. Our system also frees you from having to worry about bringing, distributing, and receiving business cards, allowing you to focus on what is truly important - making contacts that matter and generating leads.
