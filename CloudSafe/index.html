Zero-knowledge storage

Our security protocols ensure zero-knowledge storage using your existing cloud storage provider. The password for your encrypted data never leaves your own computer so nobody else, not your cloud storage provider nor us, can decrypt your data - only you can access it!

For more information on how our service keeps your data secure, click here!

Open source

Unlike many other secure cloud storage solutions, our solution is open source. Our security protocols are open for the world to scrutinize. You don't have to take our word for what is going on behind the scenes - you can check for yourself. In fact, we welcome you to contribute to the project!

We will be making the source code for our client program available here upon release!

The Protocol

Most cloud storage providers secure your data in-transit; however, the key is stored along with your data on the server. This makes your data vulnerable in the case of server compromise, unlawful access by people with access to the server, government search, and social engineering attacks.
